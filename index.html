About Fiori

Fiori is a brand that brings a cheerful, summery vibe to life through playful, fresh designs. Inspired by sunshine, nature, and color, Fiori captures the joy of warm days. The name Fiori means "flowers" in Italian, reflecting the brand's love for blooming beauty and vibrant energy. Every piece is made to brighten your day—just like summer does.
